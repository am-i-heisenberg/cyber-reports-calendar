Notes & Caveats

  • Proofpoint restructured from "State of the Phish" (single annual, ~Jan) to multi-volume "Human Factor" series in 2025. Cadence not yet established across multiple years.
  • E-ISAC GridEx reports are from a biennial exercise (odd years). Reports are typically restricted to NERC participants.
  • FS-ISAC annual reports excluded — behind membership gates with no public links.
  • Secureworks State of the Threat report merged into Sophos following Feb 2025 acquisition.
  • CISA ICS Advisories are rolling (not annual) — included as a daily monitoring source for OT/CNI.
  • IBM Cost of a Data Breach 2026 edition not yet released as of May 2026 — expected July 2026.

Compiled May 2026. For personal/team reference — not an exhaustive list.
